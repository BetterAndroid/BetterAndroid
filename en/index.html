Get Started Changelog

Minimalism First

Ditch cumbersome parameters and Intent configurations. Intuitive Kotlin extensions that shrink system interaction boilerplate into a single line.

Modernized UI

Handle WindowInsets and ViewBinding effortlessly. Native and Compose empowered to give you pixel-perfect control over multiplatform layouts.

Targeted Solutions

Focused on Android's fragmentation. Safely resolve third-party ROM quirks with a non-intrusive design, free from bloated dependencies.
